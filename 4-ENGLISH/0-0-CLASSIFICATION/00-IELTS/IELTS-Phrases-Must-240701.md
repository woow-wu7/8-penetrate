- `【 I got to go. 我得走了 】` = I have to go.
- `【 Until next time. 下次见 】`

- `【 a total of. 总共 】`

- **【 After graduation, I have 【 a total of 】 four work experiences. 毕业之后我一共经历了 4 段工作 】**
- **【 There are 【 a total of 】 one hundred employees in our company. 我们公司一共有 100 名员工 】**
