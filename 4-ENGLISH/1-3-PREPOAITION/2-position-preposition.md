# 地方介词

- `preposition 介词`
- `prep 简写`

```
in on under
next to. 在...旁边
in front of. 在...前面
behind 在...后面
--
The blue ball is on the chair. 球在椅子上面
The blue ball is under the table. 球在桌子下面
The blue ball is in the glass.
The blue ball is next to the tree. 球在树旁边
The blue ball is in front of the box. 球在盒子前面
The blue ball is behind the house. 球在房子后面
```
