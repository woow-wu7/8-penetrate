# colloquial expression 口语表达

# Before Leaving Home 离家前

```
banquet 宴会n 宴请v
necklace 项链
fabulous 极好的 adj
make-up 化妆
dress up. 穿上盛装的

Thanks for saying. 谢谢您这么说
Thanks for helping out. 谢谢你的帮忙

banquet 宴会n 宴请v
// I am on my way out to a New Year's banquet.

dress up. 穿上盛装的
// You don't have to get dressed up for this party. 你没必要为这次聚会而着盛装
```

```
Susan, good evening. Why are you so dressed up? 你怎么打扮成这样？

I am on my way out to a New Year's banquet. 我要去参加一个新年宴会。
How do I look? Is my make-up Ok ? 我看上去怎么样？我的妆可以吗？

You look great. Your make-up is perfect. 你看上去漂亮极了，你的妆也是无可挑剔的
Do you think I should wear a different dress? 你觉得我要换件礼服吗？

No, the one you have on looks fabulous. 不用，你穿这件衣服非常漂亮
Especially with your hair like that. 尤其是配着你的发型

Thanks for saying. 谢谢您这么说
Do you have any ideas which necklace I should wear? 你觉得我应该戴哪条项链呢？

With that dress I'd say your white diamond necklace would look perfect. 配这件衣服，你的白色砖石项链非常合适

Thanks for helping out. 谢谢你的帮忙
Now that I'm ready, what are you doing tonight? 我已经准备好了，你今晚干什些什么？
Not much, just a house party with some friends. 没什么，只是在家里和朋友们聚一聚
Sounds fun. Anyone I know? 听起来很有趣。有我认识的人吗？
Yeah, most of the people are from our office. 有，大多数人是我们的同事

Sounds like I'm missing out on a good time. 听起来我要错过一段开心的时刻了
Oh, well, there is always next year. 算了，还有明年呢

I'm sure you'll have fun no matter where you go. 我保证无论你去哪里都会玩的很开心的
Remember to take your bag. 记得带上你的包
```
